Nearly a Decade of Trusted Security

Dog Force Security Services (DFSS) is a Namibian private security company headquartered in Windhoek. Founded with the mission to provide reliable, professional security services, we've grown into one of the city's trusted names in private security.

With almost 10 years in the industry, our experience speaks for itself. We pride ourselves on creating a lasting impression with every client, bringing innovation to the security sector through trained security dogs and cutting-edge information technology to safeguard your assets and data.

We keep our pricing competitive while delivering the value and peace of mind that you deserve.
