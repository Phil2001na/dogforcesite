FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Home / FAQ
What areas in Windhoek do you cover?

We operate across all of Windhoek and surrounding areas including Katutura, Windhoek West, Khomasdal, Olympia, Klein Windhoek, Eros, Pioneers Park, and more.

What is your armed response time?

Our armed response teams are strategically positioned for the fastest possible response. Our 24/7 control room coordinates dispatch instantly.

Are your officers armed and certified?

Yes. All armed response officers are licensed under the Arms and Ammunition Act and registered with NAMPOL and SESORB.

How do I get CCTV monitoring set up?

Contact us for a free site assessment. We'll evaluate your property, recommend specifications, handle installation, and connect to our 24/7 control room.

Do you provide event security?

Absolutely. We cover corporate functions, weddings, festivals, concerts, and private parties with crowd management, access control, and VIP protection.

What makes DOGFORCE different?

Our K9 dog units, investment in technology, and competitive pricing without compromising quality. We're a full-spectrum security partner backed by every certification required in Namibia.

What certifications do you hold?

SESORB (Security Enterprises and Security Officers Regulation Board), SAN member, NAMPOL certified, and SSC good standing. See our Legal page for details.

Can I get a guard for just one night?

Yes. We offer long-term contracts and short-term/ad-hoc deployments. Call us to discuss your needs.

Certifications

DOGFORCE Security Services CC operates in full compliance with all Namibian security industry regulations.

SESORB Registered

Registered with the Security Enterprises and Security Officers Regulation Board as required by law.

SAN Member

Active member of the Security Association of Namibia, promoting professional standards.

NAMPOL Certified

Certified by Namibian Police. Officers comply with the Arms and Ammunition Act (No. 7 of 1996).

SSC Good Standing

Good standing with Social Security Commission under the Social Security Act, 1994.
